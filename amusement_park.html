Keansburg Beach

 

Not only are our beaches FREE, but also offer a beautiful summer breeze while overlooking the skyline of lower Manhattan.

 

  • Beaches have NO lifeguards. Swim at your own risk.
  • Firecamp and grilling are strictly prohibited.
  • Trash cans are provided. Please keep YOUR beach clean.
